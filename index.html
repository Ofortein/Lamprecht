About us


K&L Property Group is a new, dynamic and professional estate agency that recently launched in the Western Cape, South Africa. We opened our “unashamedly ethical” doors for business on the 1st March 2017.

What makes us unique is that we understand the needs of clients when it comes to buying- and selling property. For that reason, we decided to revolutionize our service offering by pro-actively giving clients what they want: a one-stop-shop for all their property needs. We acknowledge there is a gap in the industry, and we are aware that this gap is causing many frustrations to buyers and sellers alike. By closing this gap, we are committing to servicing all our clients’ needs to the fullest extent. 

Faried Karriem and Zea Lamprecht are the brains behind K&L Properties. Our extensive real estate services include:

Residential: Sale by Auction or Private Treaty
Commercial: Sale by Auction or Private Treaty
Residential Rentals
Commercial Rentals
Property Bridging
Bond Origination

We also provide value-adding support services including:

Training to all clients (Sellers and Buyers)
Compliance Inspections

We promote collaboration between different agencies in order to serve the needs of all clients:

Other Estate Agencies are able to use our resources (website, service partners, etc.)
Property workshops to share knowledge and discuss ideas to improve service delivery
